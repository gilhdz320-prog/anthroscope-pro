◆ Platform Features

Everything a Level 4 Practitioner Needs

Built from the ground up for ISAK-certified professionals. No more spreadsheet errors, manual calculations, or fragmented tools. One platform, complete precision.

◆

5-Component Ross & Kerr

Cadaver-validated model separating adipose, muscular, bone, skin, and residual mass. The gold standard for body composition analysis.

◇

Heath-Carter Somatotype

Automatic endomorphy, mesomorphy, and ectomorphy calculation with interactive somatocarta visualization for professional reports.

■

Phantom Stratagem

Z-score normalization to the 170.18cm reference phantom. Compare any athlete against 12 sport-specific global references.

◆

3D Anatomical Avatar

Three.js-powered parametric visualization of body composition. Show clients their muscle, fat, and bone distribution in real-time 3D.

◇

Genetic Muscle Potential

IMO-based ceiling calculator using Holway & Barrios (2012) norms. Know exactly how much muscle an athlete can build naturally.

■

Holway 6-Quadrant Matrix

Muscle high/low x Fat high/low classification with specific recommendations for each quadrant. Team dashboard included.

◆

Automated ETM

Technical Error of Measurement calculator with ISAK standards validation. Intra-evaluator quality control with pass/fail indicators.

◇

Professional Reports

PDF reports signed for Nivel 4, Excel exports, JSON data. Bilingual reports in English, Spanish, French, and Portuguese.

■

Group & Team Analytics

Full team evaluation with population analysis, normative comparisons, and group somatocharts for coaches and federations.
